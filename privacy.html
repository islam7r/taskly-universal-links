Privacy Policy

Last Updated: November 1, 2024

Welcome to Taskly! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and profile information you provide
  • Task and Event Data: Tasks, events, and scheduling information you create
  • Friend Connections: Information about your connections with other users
  • Usage Data: How you interact with the app, including features used and preferences
  • Device Information: Device type, operating system, and unique device identifiers
  • Push Notification Tokens: To send you notifications about tasks and events

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and improve the Taskly service
  • Send you notifications about tasks, events, and friend requests
  • Enable friend connections and collaboration features
  • Process subscription payments through Apple's In-App Purchase system
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Analyze app usage to improve our service
  • Ensure security and prevent fraud

3. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, and cloud storage
  • RevenueCat: Subscription and payment management
  • Apple Push Notification Service: Delivery of push notifications

These services have their own privacy policies governing their use of your information:

  • Firebase Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple Privacy Policy

4. Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your information:

  • With other users you choose to connect with as friends
  • With service providers who help us operate the app (Firebase, RevenueCat)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets (with prior notice)

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication through Firebase
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited access to personal information by employees

However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the following rights:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Correction: Update or correct your information in the app
  • Deletion: Delete your account and associated data at any time
  • Opt-Out: Disable push notifications in your device settings
  • Data Portability: Request your data in a portable format

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below or use the account deletion feature in the app settings.

7. Children's Privacy

Taskly is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

8. Data Retention

We retain your information:

  • While your account is active
  • As needed to provide you services
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • For up to 90 days after account deletion (for backup and security purposes)

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and stored on servers located outside your country of residence. By using Taskly, you consent to the transfer of your information to countries that may have different data protection laws.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date
  • Sending you an in-app notification for significant changes

Your continued use of Taskly after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
